Our Heritage

Tradition Meets Excellence

Every dish at Pathal Kitchen tells a story of centuries-old culinary traditions, meticulously preserved and passionately presented.

Pathal Kitchen brings the authentic flavors of Malabar to the heart of Kochi, where every grain of rice and every spice blend speaks of tradition and quality.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to serve the most authentic Malabar dum biriyani, prepared with centuries-old recipes passed down through generations. Each dish is a testament to the rich culinary heritage of Kerala, crafted with meticulous attention to detail and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

We believe in the art of slow cooking, where patience transforms premium ingredients into extraordinary culinary experiences. Our dum biriyani is not just a meal—it's a celebration of flavors, aromas, and traditions.
