Discover our story

Every Pathal Kitchen biriyani carries the warmth of a home kitchen in Malabar, lovingly recreated in the heart of Kochi.

Crafted with patience

The journey of Pathal Kitchen started with a single idea – to serve Kochi the most authentic Malabar dum biriyani, exactly the way it is cooked back home.

Each pot is layered with premium rice, expertly marinated meat, and hand-ground spices, then sealed and slow-cooked until every grain soaks in flavour.

  • Only the finest, hand-selected ingredients enter our kitchen.
  • Authentic dum technique perfected over years of practice.
  • Spice blends ground fresh daily for every batch.
  • Recipes that preserve Malabar's culinary heritage across generations.
Rooted in traditionPathal Kitchen is a tribute to the centuries-old art of Malabar biriyani making, where patience is the most important ingredient.
Every grain mattersRice is layered and steamed so gently that it remains long, fluffy, and fragrant, carrying just enough masala in every bite.
Crafted for gatheringsWhether it is a quiet family dinner or a grand celebration, our biriyani is designed to be the centrepiece of your table.
